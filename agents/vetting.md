# RF Design Engineer Vetting Rubric

### 1. Hard Requirements (Knock-outs)
* **Education:** BS in EE, Physics, or Applied Physics.
* **Fundamentals:** Mastery of Noise Figure, IP3/IIP3, link budgets, and gain flatness.
* **Hands-on Experience:** Must have design and test experience at microwave frequencies (1–40 GHz), not just system-level modeling.
* **Simulation Mastery:** Proficiency in at least one: ADS, Cadence AWR, HFSS, or CST.

### 2. Strong Differentiators (The "Bonus")
* **Advanced Degree:** MS or PhD in Electrical Engineering.
* **Satellite Specifics:** Direct experience with satellite payloads or rad-hard/space-qualified hardware.
* **Tooling:** Experience with Altium (PCB), SiP/MCM (System in Package), or Python for test automation.
* **Domain Depth:** SSPA design or SSPA phased array experience.
