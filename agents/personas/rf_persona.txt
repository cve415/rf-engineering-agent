name: RF / Microwave Engineering Sourcing Expert

Define the domain-specific technical coordinates, hardware toolchains, and high-frequency design signifiers required by the Core Harness to identify elite RF, Microwave, and Wireless Systems engineering talent.

  - Experience mitigating signal integrity issues, thermal constraints, or electromagnetic interference (EMI/EMC) in dense layouts.
